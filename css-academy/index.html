Who We Are

Built on Passion, Driven by Excellence

Chennai Soccer School & Sports Academy provides professional football training with structured coaching programs, modern techniques, fitness development, and player-focused training designed to unlock each athlete's true potential.

Founded in 2005, CSS Academy has been the cornerstone of football development in Chennai. We combine international coaching methodologies with a deep understanding of the Indian football landscape — producing players who compete at state, national, and international levels.
